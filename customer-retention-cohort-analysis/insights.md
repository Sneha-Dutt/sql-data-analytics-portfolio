Insights:

1.The dataset contains ~96K unique customers, providing a large sample for analyzing e-commerce purchasing behavior.

2.Orders increase significantly during certain months, indicating seasonal demand.

3.Top 10 customers generated R$352906.29 in total revenue and the top 25k customers contribute to 60% of the total revenue.

4.The majority of customers place only a single order, indicating low repeat purchase behavior.
Only 3.1% of customers placed more than one order.

5.Average order value remains relatively stable across customers, suggesting that revenue growth depends more on increasing purchase frequency.
The average customer lifetime value is R$166.59

6.Customers with higher lifetime value typically make multiple purchases, highlighting the importance of retention strategies.
Segmenting customers based on lifetime value could enable targeted marketing strategies to improve retention and revenue.
