Insights:

1. Approximately 96.6% of registered users placed at least one order, indicating strong conversion from registration to purchase in the customer acquisition funnel.
2. Over 96% of all orders were successfully delivered, indicating a highly efficient fulfillment pipeline with minimal order failure.
3. Only ~3% of customers made repeat purchases, revealing very low customer retention and a strong opportunity to increase revenue through loyalty programs and remarketing strategies.
4. Orders take approximately 12 days on average to reach customers, reflecting the logistics cycle across different regions.
5. Order volume increased more than 8x between early 2017 and 2018, demonstrating rapid marketplace expansion.
6. Each transaction generates roughly R$160 in revenue, suggesting the platform primarily sells mid-priced consumer products.
7. The dataset captures over R$16 million in marketplace transactions, highlighting the scale of the platform’s operations.
8. The highest-value customer spends ~75x more than the average customer, indicating strong revenue concentration among a small customer segment.
9. Customers typically place only one order, reinforcing the earlier finding of low repeat purchasing behavior.
10. Monthly revenue grew nearly 10x between early 2017 and 2018, reflecting rapid growth in marketplace demand.
11. The platform maintains a very high delivery completion rate, suggesting strong logistics coordination and order processing efficiency.
